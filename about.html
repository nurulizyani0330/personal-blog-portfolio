About Me

I'm an undergraduate Computer Science student at Universiti Sultan Zainal Abidin (UniSZA) with specialisations in Software Development. I have a strong passion in full-stack web development and intelligent algorithmic logic.


My academic research strengths are in developing impactful software that has real commercial and industrial applications. One such example is my final year project, SweepyClean, a home cleaning management system based on optimized customer assignment grids to minimize operational latency and ease the scheduling logistics for modern cleaning companies.


My digital entrepreneurship goal is to create value for the long haul by making the connection between theory and practice between the realms of computing and reality; to solve everyday technical problems and to share professional open-source code solutions with anyone, through this portfolio blog.
